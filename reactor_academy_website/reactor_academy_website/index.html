The programme arc

Students begin broad, then progressively specialise through missions, proof gates, external validation, and final ecosystem matching.

Phase 01 · Months 1–3

Frontier Discovery and Builder Formation

Move from broad curiosity to a clear frontier mission, chosen pathway, and team or working group.

Output: Builder Thesis Brief
Phase 02 · Months 4–6

Technical Proof and Knowledge Building

Turn the idea into evidence by mapping risks, building version 0, and passing the mid-year proof gate.

Output: Mid-Year Proof Package
Phase 03 · Months 7–9

Industry Validation and External Testing

Leave the classroom and test whether the market, research community, or industry actually cares.

Output: Industry Validation Report
Phase 04 · Months 10–12

Pathway Readiness and Reactor Assembly

Package the work for investors, labs, companies, mentors, and Reactor Ventures pathways.

Output: Final Builder Passport

Simple monthly rhythm

Each month repeats the same structure, so the programme feels clear instead of overwhelming.

Week 1

Frontier Briefing

Students learn from a practitioner or expert about an emerging deep tech area.

Week 2

Pathway Studio

Students apply the topic to their founder, researcher, operator/investor, or enterprise talent pathway.

Week 3

Mentor Clinic

Mentors, sponsors, researchers, founders, or investors give targeted feedback.

Week 4

Build Sprint

Students submit a small output and update their Builder Passport.

One journey, four outputs

All students move through the same 12-month system, but their evidence and final deliverables differ by pathway.

🚀

Founder Pathway

Goal: Build a deep tech startup that is ready for serious investor or pilot conversations.

  • Prototype or MVP
  • Investor pitch deck
  • Customer discovery evidence
  • Pilot plan and commercialisation roadmap
🔬

Researcher Pathway

Goal: Build frontier knowledge with a credible thesis direction and next-step lab pathway.

  • Preliminary thesis
  • Literature review and research gap
  • Methodology and expert feedback
  • Research proposal or grant/lab application
📈

Operator / Investor Pathway

Goal: Build the ecosystem around deep tech through strategy, market intelligence, and venture thinking.

  • Venture thesis
  • Investment memo
  • Market map and startup analysis
  • Operator playbook or corporate innovation recommendation
🏢

Enterprise Talent Pathway

Goal: Secure a deep tech career at an industry sponsor such as Google, Micron, or Nvidia.

  • Corporate challenge portfolio
  • Verified technical skills dashboard
  • Externship or mini-internship report
  • Final interview presentation

Final event · Month 12

Reactor Assembly: Frontier Proof-of-Work Demo Day

This is not a normal graduation ceremony. It is an ecosystem-matching event where students show validated work to industry sponsors, corporate partners, Reactor Ventures, investors, researchers, founders, mentors, and alumni.

Founder students pitchStartup problem, prototype, market validation, technical defensibility, pilot plan, and investment ask.
Researcher students presentPreliminary thesis, research gap, methodology, expert feedback, and possible lab or grant pathway.
Operator / investor students shareVenture thesis, investment memo, market map, startup analysis, and strategic recommendation.
Enterprise talent students presentCorporate challenge portfolio, verified technical skills dashboard, externship learnings, and final job or internship ask.

What companies get out of Reactor Academy

Reactor Academy gives companies early access to ambitious frontier talent before they enter the traditional graduate market.
